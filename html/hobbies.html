Boxing

I practiced boxing for two years, a discipline that taught me a great deal, both physically and mentally. Even though I no longer train as regularly today, my father — who has a great passion for this sport — still trains me from time to time, which allows me to keep the pace and progress at my own rhythm. As a Kazakh, boxing holds a special place in my heart. It is a very popular sport in Kazakhstan, with many world-renowned champions such as Gennady Golovkin, who have put our country on the international stage. This sporting culture is part of my identity and inspires me every day in everything I do.

Crochet

I love crochet for its blend of creativity and relaxation. Nothing pleases me more than transforming a simple thread into useful and personalised objects, like book sleeves (perfect for protecting and brightening up favourite reads) or unique keychains (ideal for adding a handmade touch to everyday life). Every stitch counts, and it is this attention to detail that makes each creation so satisfying to make and to give.

Cultures

Beyond sport, it is the richness of my culture that I love to discover and share. I enjoy learning more about my roots, our traditions, our history, our language. At the same time, I grew up in France, a country I deeply love. French culture is also part of me. It shaped me, inspires me, and has offered me many opportunities. Today, I see myself at the crossroads of two worlds: Kazakhstan and France. These two cultures complement each other, they form my identity, and I truly enjoy learning and growing between the two.

Programming

Beyond my studies, programming is a genuine passion of mine. I enjoy building projects from scratch and exploring new technologies in my spare time. Whether it is web development, data analysis, or experimenting with new frameworks, I love the challenge of turning ideas into working software. Programming is not just a skill for me — it is a hobby that keeps me curious and creative.
